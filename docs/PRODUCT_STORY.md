# Product Story and Business Case

## Problem

B2B operations teams often receive recurring client questions that are already known internally, but the answer is scattered across old tickets, SOPs, and team memory.

## Hypothesis

An AI copilot can reduce handling time by:
- retrieving likely precedent cases;
- proposing the next operational step;
- drafting a response;
- creating high-quality escalation tickets only when needed.

## User personas

### 1. Frontline operations specialist
Needs speed and confidence while handling incoming client issues.

### 2. Team lead / escalation manager
Needs cleaner handoffs, fewer weak tickets, and visibility into recurring friction.

### 3. Transformation / PMO lead
Needs a repeatable way to identify, measure, and prioritize automations.

## KPIs

- First-touch resolution rate
- Ticket deflection rate
- Escalation precision
- Average time to triage
- Average number of back-and-forth clarification messages
- Recurrence of top issue categories

## Why the project is stronger than a chatbot

A chatbot demonstrates language capability. This project demonstrates **operational leverage**:
- a workflow,
- a decision policy,
- a human handoff,
- and measurable process impact.
